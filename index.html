About AMTUS Philippines

AMTUS Philippines is a dedicated tutorial service provider focused on helping students build strong academic foundations. We offer personalized learning experiences tailored to each student's needs, ensuring effective understanding, confidence, and academic growth. Our tutors are committed, passionate, and equipped to guide learners in both online and face-to-face settings.

Vision

To become a leading tutorial service provider in the Philippines, nurturing confident, competent, and lifelong learners.

Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality, and personalized tutorial services that strengthen academic skills and foster a love for learning.

Services Offered

  • 📘 One-on-One Tutorial (Online & Face-to-Face)
  • 👥 Group Tutorial Sessions
  • 🧮 Math Fundamentals (Grades 1–6)
  • 📖 Reading & English Support
  • 📝 Homework Assistance
  • 🎯 Customized Learning Plans

Registration Process

  • Step 1: Inquiry – Send your details (Student Name, Grade Level, Subject, Preferred Schedule)
  • Step 2: Assessment & Matching – We assess your needs and match you with the right tutor
  • Step 3: Start Learning – Begin your personalized tutorial sessions
